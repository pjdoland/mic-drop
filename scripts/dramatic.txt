In the beginning, there was silence.
Then, from the deepest corners of the void, a voice emerged.
It spoke with a clarity that shattered the stillness around it.
"You will not be forgotten," it said, its words carrying the weight of centuries.
The world held its breath, waiting for what would come next.
And what came next changed everything.

Not with thunder. Not with fire. But with a single, quiet sentence
that rewrote the story everyone thought they already knew.

This is that sentence.
